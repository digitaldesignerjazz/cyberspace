# Contributing to Nexus Cyberspace

This repository focuses on the **immersive, creative, and narrative layer** of the Nexus ecosystem. It explores virtual worlds, cyberpunk and fantasy environments, agent-driven storytelling, and immersive experiences built on top of the decentralized technical foundation (Nexus orchestration, QNET blockchain, and Xanadu/xmesh communication).

## Code of Conduct

We welcome writers, world-builders, game designers, narrative designers, AI prompt engineers, roleplayers, and technologists interested in the intersection of immersive storytelling and decentralized systems. Respect, creativity, and constructive collaboration are core values.

## How to Contribute

### Areas of Contribution

**World Building**
- Designing cyberpunk, fantasy, or hybrid worlds
- Defining lore, factions, locations, and rules of the world
- Creating consistent world bibles and setting documents

**Narrative & Storytelling**
- Agent-driven narratives and interactive stories
- Quest and mission design for AI agents
- Branching narratives and emergent storytelling

**Immersive Experiences**
- Concepts for immersive scenarios and roleplay sessions
- Integration of Suno music, visual descriptions, and sensory elements
- Designing experiences that leverage the decentralized mesh and blockchain layers

**Agent Personas & Roles**
- Creating rich, consistent AI agent personas that inhabit the cyberspace
- Defining motivations, backstories, and behavioral patterns
- Exploring how agents interact across layers (Nexus orchestration, QNET incentives, Xanadu communication)

**Technical Integration Concepts**
- Ideas for how immersive experiences can interface with Nexus, QNET, and Xanadu/xmesh
- Concepts for on-chain narrative elements or reputation systems that affect storytelling
- Documentation of creative-technical bridges

**Documentation & Lore**
- Writing high-quality documentation, wikis, and setting guides
- Creating visual maps, diagrams, and mood boards

## Style Guidelines
- Maintain internal consistency within worlds and narratives
- Clearly separate creative fiction from technical specifications
- When proposing technical integration, explain how it connects to Nexus, QNET, or Xanadu/xmesh

## Recognition
Contributors will be credited in documentation and releases. Significant creative contributions may be highlighted in immersive experiences or future projects.
